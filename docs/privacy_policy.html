Privacy Policy for Platiša

Last updated: January 2, 2026

Platiša ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application uses your information, specifically regarding the integration with Google Gmail APIs.

1. Information We Collect

Our application accesses the following information only when you explicitly grant permission:

  • Gmail Messages & Attachments: We access your emails solely to identify, download, and extract utility bills (e.g., electricity, phone, internet details).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the accessed data for the following specific purposes:

  • To identify emails that contain PDF invoices or bills.
  • To parse these bills extracting relevant details such as the Merchant Name, Total Amount Due, and Billing Period using automated processing.
  • To organize and display this information within the Platiša app to help you track your monthly expenses.

We do NOT use your email data for advertising purposes. No human sees your emails; all processing is automated on your device.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local Processing: All parsing and extraction happen locally on your device.
  • Local Storage: Extracted bill data is stored locally in the application's secure database on your device.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not transfer, sell, or share your email data or personal information with any third-party services, advertisers, or external servers.

4. Limited Use Policy

Platiša's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the app's access to your Gmail account at any time via your Google Account security settings.
  • Delete Data: You can delete all stored bill data by clearing the app's storage in your Android settings or by uninstalling the application.
